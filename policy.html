EDGE Solutions – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 19, 2025

At EDGE Solutions, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our applications, websites, or IT services (including servers, networking, storage, and automation solutions), as well as integrations with the Facebook Graph API.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data depending on the services you use:

  • From our IT services: contact information, company details, project requirements, and technical specifications for servers, networks, storage, and automation systems.
  • From Facebook Graph API integrations: basic profile details (name, email, profile picture) and other information you explicitly authorize.
  • Usage data: logs, diagnostics, and metadata necessary to ensure secure and reliable service delivery.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us deliver secure, reliable, and tailored solutions. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Provide IT infrastructure solutions (servers, networks, storage, automation).
  • Authenticate access and enable login through Facebook (when integrations are used).
  • Improve the performance, security, and reliability of our services.
  • Communicate updates, support, or service notices.
  • Comply with legal and contractual obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your information. Data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted partners or subcontractors who support our IT projects, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • With Facebook, when you explicitly use our integrations, in compliance with their Platform Policies.
  • As required by applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Security

EDGE Solutions takes security seriously. We apply industry best practices in encryption, network hardening, and infrastructure protection to safeguard your information. While no system is entirely risk-free, we continuously monitor and improve our security measures.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services and meet compliance obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for business use and not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
